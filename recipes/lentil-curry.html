Lentil Curry

Description

My favorite meals are delicious mush. Curry makes my soul feel warm. This is an easy, one-pot dish that will have people talking. Share this with friends. Serve with naan or bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of green lentils
  • half of a large onion
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp curry paste
  • 1 Tbsp turmeric
  • 2 Tbsp cumin
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Dice the half onion. Cook in oil until tender and brown.
  2. Add curry paste and spices, mix until fragrant.
  3. Add all canned ingredients and lentils. Bring to a boil.
  4. Stir for 40-60 minutes or until lentils are tender.
